# Board Member Guide - Bramhollow Condominium System

*Specialized guide for Board of Directors members*

## Board Member Role Overview

### Your Enhanced Access

As a Board Member, you have expanded privileges beyond regular residents:

**Additional Capabilities:**
- Create and manage community events
- Access board-specific documents and reports
- View extended community analytics
- Participate in board-only announcements
- Enhanced service request visibility
- Access to financial summaries and reports

**Your Responsibilities:**
- Represent resident interests effectively
- Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information
- Participate in community decision-making
- Support building management initiatives
- Communicate board decisions to residents

---

## Board-Specific Features

### Event Management

**Creating Community Events:**
1. Navigate to **Events** tab
2. Click **"Create Event"** (board members only)
3. Complete event details:
   - **Event Title**: Clear, descriptive name
   - **Date & Time**: Use date/time picker for accuracy
   - **Location**: Specify exact location (community room, lobby, etc.)
   - **Description**: Include agenda, purpose, or activities
   - **RSVP Required**: Choose if headcount needed
   - **Board Only**: Select if event is for board members only

**Event Management Best Practices:**
- **Plan ahead**: Create events at least 2 weeks in advance
- **Clear communication**: Include all necessary details
- **RSVP tracking**: Monitor attendance for planning
- **Follow-up**: Send reminders closer to event date

### Document Access

**Board Document Categories:**
- **Meeting Minutes**: Monthly and annual meeting records
- **Financial Reports**: Budget summaries and financial statements
- **Policy Documents**: Board resolutions and policy changes
- **Legal Documents**: Contracts, agreements, and legal notices
- **Strategic Plans**: Long-term planning and development documents

**Document Security:**
- Board documents are confidential
- Do not share outside board without authorization
- Access is logged for security purposes
- Report any concerns about document access

### Communication Responsibilities

**Board Announcements:**
Board members can help draft and review announcements:
- Review drafts created by management
- Suggest improvements or additions
- Ensure board perspective is represented
- Verify accuracy of board-related information

**Resident Communication:**
- Respond to resident questions professionally
- Direct complex issues to management
- Maintain consistent messaging with other board members
- Refer policy questions to official channels

## Resident Relations

### Addressing Resident Concerns

**Common Issues Board Members Handle:**
- Policy questions and interpretations
- Neighbor disputes and mediation
- Building maintenance concerns
- Community event suggestions
- Financial questions about assessments

**Response Guidelines:**
1. **Listen carefully** to resident concerns
2. **Gather complete information** before responding
3. **Consult other board members** on policy matters
4. **Refer to management** for operational issues
5. **Follow up** to ensure resolution

### Communication Best Practices

**Do:**
- Maintain professional tone in all communications
- Respond to resident inquiries promptly
- Keep confidential information private
- Present unified board positions
- Direct residents to appropriate resources

**Don't:**
- Make commitments without full board discussion
- Share confidential financial or legal information
- Discuss personnel matters with residents
- Make policy decisions unilaterally
- Ignore or dismiss resident concerns

## Emergency Procedures

### Board Role in Emergencies

**Emergency Response:**
- Support management during building emergencies
- Communicate with residents during crises
- Make emergency decisions when necessary
- Coordinate with emergency services as needed

**Types of Emergencies:**
- Building system failures (power, water, HVAC)
- Weather-related emergencies
- Security incidents
- Health and safety emergencies

### Crisis Communication

**Communication Protocol:**
1. **Immediate safety**: Ensure resident safety first
2. **Information gathering**: Get accurate information quickly
3. **Resident notification**: Use multiple channels to communicate
4. **Regular updates**: Keep residents informed of progress
5. **Follow-up**: Document incident and improve procedures
