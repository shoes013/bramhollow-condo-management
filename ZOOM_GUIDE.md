# Bramhollow Condominium Association
## Zoom Meeting Guide for Members

**Last Updated:** January 10, 2026

---

## Overview

All Bramhollow Condominium Association meetings are now available via Zoom videoconference. This allows members to participate in board meetings, annual meetings, and community events from anywhere.

**Per N.J.S.A. 46:8B-12, all board meetings remain open to unit owners. Virtual attendance ensures accessibility for all members.**

---

## Getting Started with Zoom

### Step 1: Download Zoom

Visit [zoom.us/download](https://zoom.us/download) to download Zoom for your device:

- **Windows PC or Mac**: Download "Zoom Desktop Client"
- **iPhone/iPad**: Download from the App Store
- **Android**: Download from Google Play Store

### Step 2: Create a Free Account (Optional)

While you can join meetings without an account, we recommend creating a free Zoom account:

1. Go to [zoom.us/signup](https://zoom.us/signup)
2. Enter your email address
3. Follow the verification steps
4. Set up your profile

### Step 3: Test Your Setup

Before your first meeting:

1. Open the Zoom app
2. Click "Join a Test Meeting" or visit [zoom.us/test](https://zoom.us/test)
3. Test your audio (speakers and microphone)
4. Test your video (camera)

## How to Join a Bramhollow Meeting

### Option 1: Click the Zoom Link

1. Log in to the Bramhollow Community Portal
2. Go to the **Events** tab
3. Find your meeting and click **"Join Zoom Meeting"**
4. Zoom will open automatically

### Option 2: Enter Meeting ID Manually

1. Open the Zoom app
2. Click **"Join"**
3. Enter the **Meeting ID** (found on the Events page)
4. Enter your name
5. Click **"Join"**
6. Enter the **Passcode** when prompted

## Meeting Etiquette

### Before the Meeting

- Join 5-10 minutes early to troubleshoot any issues
- Find a quiet location with good lighting
- Use headphones if possible for better audio

### During the Meeting

- **Mute yourself** when not speaking (click the microphone icon)
- **Raise your hand** to speak (click "Reactions" then "Raise Hand")
- Use **Chat** for questions if the meeting host requests
- Keep your camera on if comfortable, but it's optional

### Technical Issues

If you experience problems:
- Check your internet connection
- Restart the Zoom app
- Try joining by phone (dial-in numbers provided in meeting invite)
- Contact the management office for assistance

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to pay for Zoom?

No. The free version of Zoom allows you to join meetings of any length. Only meeting hosts need paid accounts.

### Can I join from my phone?

Yes! Download the Zoom app from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android), or dial in using the phone numbers provided.

### What if I don't have a camera?

You can still participate. Audio-only participation is welcome.

### How do I vote during meetings?

The meeting host will provide instructions. Typically, voting is done by:
- Raising your hand feature
- Responding in chat
- Verbal roll call

### What if I have technical issues during the meeting?

- Try leaving and rejoining the meeting
- Use the chat to notify the host
- Call the management office for assistance
