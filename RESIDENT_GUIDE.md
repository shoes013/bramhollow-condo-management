## Service Requests

### When to Submit Service Requests

**Maintenance Issues:**
- Plumbing problems (leaks, clogs, pressure issues)
- Electrical issues (outlets, lighting, circuit breakers)
- HVAC problems (heating, cooling, ventilation)
- Appliance malfunctions in common areas
- Structural concerns (cracks, settling, damage)

**Security Concerns:**
- Broken locks or access control issues
- Lighting problems in common areas
- Security camera or intercom malfunctions
- Suspicious activity or safety hazards
- Emergency exit or fire safety equipment issues

**Amenity Problems:**
- Pool, gym, or recreation area issues
- Common room equipment problems
- Laundry facility malfunctions
- Parking area concerns
- Landscaping or exterior maintenance

**Administrative Requests:**
- Document requests and policy questions
- Address changes or contact updates
- Guest registration or parking permits
- Insurance or financial inquiries
- Move-in/move-out coordination

### How to Submit Effective Service Requests

**Step-by-Step Process:**
1. **Navigate to Service Requests Tab**
2. **Click "New Request" Button**
3. **Complete the Form:**
   - **Title**: Brief, clear description of the issue
   - **Category**: Select the most appropriate category
   - **Priority**: Choose based on urgency and safety
   - **Description**: Provide detailed information
4. **Submit and Save Confirmation Number**

**Writing Effective Descriptions:**
- **Be specific**: Include exact location and unit number
- **Include details**: When did you first notice the problem?
- **Describe symptoms**: What exactly is happening?
- **Note safety concerns**: Is anyone at risk?
- **Include contact preferences**: Best time and method to reach you

**Priority Guidelines:**
- **High Priority**: Safety hazards, water leaks, security issues, no heat/AC
- **Medium Priority**: Non-emergency repairs, noise complaints, minor leaks
- **Low Priority**: Cosmetic issues, suggestions, non-urgent maintenance

### Tracking Your Service Requests

**Status Meanings:**
- 🟡 **Pending**: Request received, awaiting review and assignment
- 🔵 **In Progress**: Work has been assigned and is underway
- 🟢 **Completed**: Issue has been resolved
- 🔴 **Cancelled**: Request withdrawn or no longer needed

**What to Expect:**
- **Acknowledgment**: Confirmation within 1 business day
- **Status Updates**: Regular updates on progress
- **Completion Notice**: Notification when work is finished
- **Follow-up**: Opportunity to confirm satisfaction

**If You're Not Satisfied:**
- Contact management if work seems incomplete
- Request follow-up if problem persists
- Provide feedback on service quality
- Submit new request if additional issues arise

## Safety and Emergency Procedures

### Building Safety Features

**Fire Safety:**
- Smoke detectors and fire alarms in all units
- Emergency exits clearly marked
- Fire extinguishers in common areas
- Sprinkler systems (if installed)

**Security Systems:**
- Controlled access entry systems
- Security cameras in common areas
- Emergency communication systems
- Lighting in all common areas

### Emergency Procedures

**Fire Emergency:**
1. **Evacuate Immediately** using nearest exit
2. **Call 911** once safely outside
3. **Meet at Designated** assembly area
4. **Don't Re-enter** until cleared by fire department

**Medical Emergency:**
1. **Call 911** for serious medical situations
2. **Notify Building Management** if possible
3. **Provide Access** for emergency responders
4. **Follow Medical Professional** instructions

**Security Concerns:**
1. **Call 911** for immediate threats
2. **Contact Building Management** for non-emergency security issues
3. **Document Details** of suspicious activity
4. **Report to Board** ongoing security concerns

**Utility Emergencies:**
1. **Water Leaks**: Shut off water if possible, call management immediately
2. **Power Outages**: Check with neighbors, report to utility company
3. **Gas Leaks**: Evacuate immediately, call gas company and 911
4. **Elevator Problems**: Use emergency phone, don't force doors
